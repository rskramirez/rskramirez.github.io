In Loving Memory of Mateo Garcia

Date of Birth: 1st August 1947 - Date of Passing: 26th April 2024

Occupation: Environmental Scientist

Education: Bachelor's Degree in Environmental Science from the University of Costa Rica; Master's Degree in Sustainable Development from Stanford University

Personal Interests: Passionate about wildlife conservation, avid traveler with a love for exploring national parks, and an amateur photographer with a particular interest in capturing nature.

Achievements: Played a pivotal role in several successful conservation projects in Central America; published articles on sustainable practices and environmental protection in various scientific journals.

Family: Survived by his spouse, Clara Garcia, and two children, Sofia and Luis. He is remembered fondly by his extended family and close friends.

Legacy: Known for his dedication to environmental issues and his ability to inspire others to take action. His contributions to the field of environmental science have left a lasting impact on his community and beyond.
